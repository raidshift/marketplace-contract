  • RAIDSHIFT marketplace is an order book based decentralized Peer-to-Peer token exchange on TRON Blockchain.
  • Its ownerless immutable Smart Contracts have a small footprint to keep transaction fees low.
  • Exchanging tokens with peers is free of charge.
  • You can vote with your RFS tokens for future token/USDT trading pairs to be added to the platform. Voting requires you to provide a TRC20-token address and the amount of RFS tokens (= votes) you want to spend.
  • RFS tokens spent for voting will be burned by the voting contract.
