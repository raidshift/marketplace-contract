About RAIDSHIFT marketplace
  • RAIDSHIFT marketplace is a decentralized Peer-to-Peer crypto token exchange on TRON Blockchain.
  • Its Smart Contracts have a very small footprint with no owner or governance to prevent frauds and keep transaction fees as low as possible.
  • Exchanging tokens with peers is free of charge.
